How it works

Build, Organize and Link Product Content & Data

1

Build Content

You gain competitive advantage by giving customers all the data accessible about an item. They can seek or explore rapidly the product to see content

2

Product Categorization

Develop a customer focused taxonomy with clear menu labels, navigation filters and paths, attribute names, product titles, feature/benefits lists, and specifications.

3

Data Cleansing

Identification of quality issues, elimating duplicates, idenify data elements in master data structure and map existing data to new master data structure.

4
