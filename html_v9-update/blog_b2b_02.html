Let’s go eCommerce for the display of B2B products!

  • Aug 30, 2017
  • by Admin
  • 5 Comments

The perfect leveraging step for manufacturers and B2B firms is to display their wide range of products to a broader audience. Traditionally, the wholesale businesses provide information for their customers via catalogs and upon product enquiry. But, the trendy ones are catching-up with the tech-savvy consumers of today. It is essential for B2B companies to provide their buyers with complete product information that is easily accessible online.

Digitalize your product catalogs to cater direct information

Product catalogs are definitely one of the most important assets that provides all the information from the manufacturers. But, looking a little further, the clients might have to go over multiple pages and sort through a lot of data to get the essential information they need. So, to create a user-friendly and efficient approach for the customers, a separate landing page for each product with complete product details and specifications will be an excellent solution. This gives your customers the same experience of buying from any B2C online store.

Master Data Management (MDM) system

Having a digitalized online product catalog gives the B2B businesses the advantage of maintaining all their product information in one place. This also works as a Master Data Management (MDM) system, where all the products and their relevant information can be maintained. Both the in-house teams and the customers located in any region will have quick and easy access to the entire array of product details.

Creating the Possibility of ordering products online

For businesses that don’t have the option of ordering online yet, eCommerce opens doors. Customers will be able to order products online in bulk and add to their wish list. Additionally, all information and transactions related to each product is maintained in a structured and organized way. Inevitably this increases your sales percentages and shows new hope across all wholesale industries.
